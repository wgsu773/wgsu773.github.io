    WGSU 773 Girl Scouts

    Project maintained by wgsu773 Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    We are the Girl Scouts of West Geauga Service Unit 773, serving the schools of the West Geauga school district and Saint Anselm, grades K-12, in Northeast Ohio.

    Girl Scout Mission

    Girl Scouting builds girls of courage, confidence, and character, who make the world a better place.

    Girl Scout Promise

    On my honor, I will try: To serve God and my country, To help people at all times, And to live by the Girl Scout Law.

    Girl Scout Law

    I will do my best to be honest and fair, friendly and helpful, considerate and caring, courageous and strong, and responsible for what I say and do, and to respect myself and others, respect authority, use resources wisely, make the world a better place, and be a sister to every Girl Scout.
